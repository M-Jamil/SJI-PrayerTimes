
SJI Prayer Times (Athan/Azan) for Mobile & Offline Masjid (Mosque) Display

SJI Prayer Times is a fully offline Islamic prayer times app designed for both Mobile and Masjid displays.

Key Features

- Masjid display can work completely offline with its own built-in clock.
- Displays prayer times calculated automatically or loaded from custom yearly timetable (CSV)
- Plays Athan on prayer time with notifications.
- Customizable PDF timetable to share monthly prayer time schedule.
- Works completely offline with its own built-in clock (no internet required)
- Manual adjustment of Athan & Iqamah times if needed
- Backward countdown for both Athan and Iqamah
- Supports both Gregorian and Hijri calendars
- Export monthly prayer timetables as PDF for easy sharing and printing

Supported Devices

- Android phones and tablets (Android 7.0 and above)
- Android TV and TV boxes
- Amazon Fire TV (supported)
